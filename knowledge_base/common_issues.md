# Common Issues - Quick Reference Guide

Last Updated: February 2024

## Overview
This document provides quick solutions for the most frequently encountered support issues. For detailed troubleshooting, see the individual guides linked below.

---

## Network Connectivity Issues

### Issue: No 5G Connection
**Quick Fix:**
1. Toggle Airplane Mode on/off
2. Check if 5G is enabled: Settings → Mobile Data → Enable 5G
3. Restart device

**Escalate if:** Issue persists after 3 attempts
**Average Resolution Time:** 15 minutes
**Related Guide:** [Network Connectivity Troubleshooting](troubleshooting_guides/network_connectivity.md)

---

### Issue: Slow Data Speeds
**Quick Fix:**
1. Check network coverage area
2. Run speed test to confirm issue
3. Reset network settings: Settings → General → Reset → Reset Network Settings

**Escalate if:** Speeds consistently below 10 Mbps in good coverage area
**Average Resolution Time:** 20 minutes

---

## Device Activation Issues

### Issue: SIM Card Not Detected
**Quick Fix:**
1. Power off device completely
2. Remove and reseat SIM card
3. Clean SIM card contacts with dry cloth
4. Reinsert and power on

**Escalate if:** SIM not detected after reseating
**Average Resolution Time:** 10 minutes
**Related Guide:** [Device Activation Guide](troubleshooting_guides/device_activation.md)

---

### Issue: Activation Error Code 404
**Quick Fix:**
1. Verify IMEI matches order: Dial *#06#
2. Check SIM provisioning status in system
3. Manual activation: *#*#4636#*#*

**Escalate if:** IMEI mismatch or provisioning errors
**Average Resolution Time:** 25 minutes

---

## Billing Issues

### Issue: Unexpected Roaming Charges
**Quick Fix:**
1. Verify travel dates and location
2. Check roaming settings were enabled
3. Review call detail records for location

**Escalate if:** Customer disputes location data
**Average Resolution Time:** 30 minutes
**Related Guide:** [Billing Issue Resolution](troubleshooting_guides/billing_issues.md)

---

### Issue: Payment Method Update
**Quick Fix:**
1. Direct customer to online account portal
2. Guide through: Account → Payment Methods → Add/Update
3. Verify new payment method is primary

**Escalate if:** Payment portal errors
**Average Resolution Time:** 10 minutes

---

## 5G Home Internet Issues

### Issue: Gateway Not Powering On
**Quick Fix:**
1. Try different power outlet
2. Check power adapter connection
3. Look for any LED activity

**Escalate if:** No LED activity after trying 3 different outlets (likely hardware failure)
**Average Resolution Time:** 15 minutes

---

### Issue: Weak Signal Strength
**Quick Fix:**
1. Move gateway to window facing nearest tower
2. Rotate gateway 90 degrees
3. Elevate gateway (higher placement = better signal)
4. Check signal bars in gateway admin panel

**Escalate if:** Signal bars remain at 1-2 after repositioning
**Average Resolution Time:** 20 minutes

## Escalation Criteria

### When to Escalate to Engineering:

- ✅ Hardware suspected to be defective
- ✅ Tower/network outage affecting multiple customers
- ✅ Account provisioning errors in backend systems
- ✅ Billing disputes requiring management approval
- ✅ Issues unresolved after 3 troubleshooting attempts

### When to Escalate to Billing:

- ✅ Refund requests over $50
- ✅ Account credit disputes
- ✅ Payment processing failures

---

## Documentation Standards

When documenting tickets, always include:

1. **Symptoms:** Exact error messages, when issue started
2. **Troubleshooting Steps:** List everything attempted
3. **Resolution:** Specific actions that fixed the issue
4. **Follow-up:** Any pending actions or customer commitments
